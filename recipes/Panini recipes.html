Panini Cooking and Recipes

 

  • Ingredients of Panini

  • 1 tablespoon extra-light olive oil, or as needed.
    2 slices Italian bread.
    2 tablespoons mayonnaise.
    3 slices deli ham, or more to taste.
    2 slices Cheddar cheese.
    1 slice firm-ripe tomato.
    1 thin slice onion.
    cooking spray.

How to make Panini

  • Preparing panini is super easy. You split a sandwich of your choice in half, stuff it with the chosen filling, close it and grill it in the panini grill until the filling is warm. With such a simple method of preparation, it’s important to use the tastiest ingredients,
